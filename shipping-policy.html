Shipping Policy

Delivery Timeframes

Our goal is to get your order to you as quickly as possible. As we source high-quality jerseys from various locations, delivery times can range from 2 days to 4 weeks. This depends on stock availability and the origin of the item.

We appreciate your patience and understanding as we ensure you receive the best products.

Our Delivery Guarantee

We stand by our service. If, for any reason, your order is not delivered to your doorstep within 4 weeks (28 days) from the date of your purchase, we believe you deserve compensation for the delay.

In such cases, you are eligible to request a discount on your order. This can be in the form of a partial refund or a discount code for a future purchase.

How to Claim

  • Once your order has passed the 4-week delivery window, please contact our support team.
  • Provide your order number and a brief message regarding the delay.
  • Our team will verify the delivery status and promptly assist you with your compensation.

Contact Us
